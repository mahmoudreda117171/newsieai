## 📚 More Information

### How newsieai works

The app uses two main agents.

- **Profile Builder Agent**: It adjusts your profile based on what you say and your feedback. This keeps your news fresh and relevant.

- **Retrieval Agent**: It looks for news from social media channels like Twitter (X), web sources, and topic feeds. It organizes what it finds for easy browsing.

- **Accountant Agent**: It manages payments. Currently, it is not secure but planned for future updates.

### Supported content types

newsieai focuses on:

- Social media news (X/Twitter)
- Industry-specific feeds
- Paginated and paid news articles

### How payments work

Payments are verified using a blockchain-based system called Solana. This makes sure you only pay for content once and gain immediate access.
